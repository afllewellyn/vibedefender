# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: September 2025**

## Information We Collect

### Website URLs and Target Information

When you use our scanning service, we collect and temporarily process:
- Website URLs and domain information submitted for scanning
- HTTP header responses and server configuration data
- Security configuration information from target websites
- Scan timestamps and frequency data

**Important:** We do not access, collect, or store any content, user data, or private information from scanned websites beyond standard HTTP headers and publicly available security configuration data.

### Account Information

If you create an account, we collect your email address and encrypted password. We may also collect optional profile information you choose to provide.

### Scan Results

We store the results of security scans to provide you with historical data and trending analysis. This includes security findings, OWASP and CVSS-based scores, recommendations, and timestamps.

### Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our service, including:
- Pages visited and features used
- Scan frequency and patterns
- IP addresses and session information
- Browser and device information
- Error logs and performance data

### Legal Compliance Data

For security and legal compliance purposes, we may collect and retain:
- User IP addresses associated with scan requests
- Detailed scan logs including target URLs and timestamps
- Account activity logs
- Terms of Service acceptance records and versions

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and improve our security scanning services
- Maintain and analyze your scan history and trends
- Send you important service updates and security notifications
- Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized scanning activities
- Comply with legal obligations and law enforcement requests
- Monitor service performance and security
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## Legal Compliance and Data Processing

- We process website data only as necessary to provide security scanning services
- **We do not verify user authorization to scan submitted websites** - this is solely the user's responsibility
- We may be required to provide scan logs, user data, or activity records in response to legal requests
- We reserve the right to refuse service or report suspected unauthorized scanning activities to appropriate authorities
- Scan data and user activity logs may be retained for security monitoring, abuse prevention, and legal compliance purposes

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

### Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, conducting business, or serving users, as long as they agree to keep this information confidential and use it only for the specified purposes.

### Legal Enforcement and Requirements

We may disclose user information, scan data, and activity logs when:
- Required by law, legal process, court order, or government regulation
- Requested by law enforcement agencies investigating potential computer crimes
- Necessary to investigate suspected unauthorized scanning activities or Terms of Service violations
- Required to comply with national security, public safety, or regulatory requests
- Necessary to protect our legal rights, prevent illegal activity, or ensure user safety
- Required to respond to claims that user activities violate third-party rights

### Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your personal information becomes subject to different privacy practices.

### Emergency Situations

We may disclose information without notice in emergency situations to protect the safety and security of users, the public, or our services.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
- Regular security assessments, penetration testing, and vulnerability management
- Multi-factor authentication and access controls for administrative accounts
- Secure data centers with physical and logical access restrictions
- Regular employee training on data protection and security best practices
- Incident response procedures for data breaches and security events
- Regular backup and disaster recovery testing

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. Specifically:

- **Account information:** Until you delete your account or request deletion
- **Scan results and history:** Up to 2 years from the scan date for active accounts
- **Usage and activity logs:** Up to 1 year for operational and security purposes
- **Guest scan data:** 30 days from scan completion
- **Legal compliance data:** As required by applicable laws or legitimate business needs
- **Security incident data:** Up to 3 years for investigation and prevention purposes

We may retain certain information for longer periods when required by law, for legitimate business purposes, or to resolve disputes.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR, CCPA), you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access:** Request access to your personal information and scan data
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Portability:** Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection:** Object to certain processing of your information
- **Restriction:** Request restriction of processing under certain circumstances
- **Withdrawal of Consent:** Withdraw consent for processing where consent is the legal basis

To exercise these rights, please contact us via our Contact page. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

**Note:** Some rights may be limited by legal requirements, legitimate business interests, or technical constraints. For example, we may be required to retain certain data for legal compliance or security purposes.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. These include:

- **Essential cookies:** Required for basic platform functionality, security, and user authentication
- **Analytics cookies:** Help us understand how you use our service and identify areas for improvement
- **Preference cookies:** Remember your settings, preferences, and configuration choices
- **Security cookies:** Help detect suspicious activity and protect against fraud

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, though disabling certain cookies may limit platform functionality.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
